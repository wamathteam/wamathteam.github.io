The Math Team

The Westford Academy Math Team is a club focused on teaching math techniques, practicing for math contests, and competing as individuals and as a team. During the 2012-2013 school year, the Westford Academy math team advanced all the way to the New England Association of Math Leagues competition, an invitational contest for the top scorers in the invitational state-wide math contests.

The math team meets every Wednesday after school in Mrs. Gartner's room. Anyone interested in joining may attend.

Math meets are held on the first Thursday of each month. Permission slips and bus fees must be submitted before the meet on the date specified in order to attend.
